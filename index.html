About the Board

A platform, not a title.

The Wharton Tech Club is one of the largest professional clubs on campus — 400+ members, a flagship annual conference, a podcast, and a network that reaches across the industry. This year we're rebuilding the board to be leaner and more impactful: ~10 second-year VPs who own the strategy, and ~10 first-year members recruited in the fall who execute alongside them. If you're joining, you're not filling a seat — you're co-authoring what this club becomes.

How we'll work together

Before the year starts, we'll sit down with every VP and co-write a plan for the semester — event ideas, initiatives, rough timelines. These aren't mandates. They're starting points. You own the domain; we're here to help you think it through before you're in the thick of it.

What we expect

High commitment.
Real ownership.
